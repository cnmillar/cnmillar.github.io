I spent six years as co-founder and COO of Indiegraf, helping build a platform used by more than 250 independent news publishers across North America. Before that, I was Chief Data Officer and Director of Digital and Data Journalism at The Discourse, and I've worked as an investigative journalist and data journalist, including recognition through the Edward R. Murrow Award and as a Knight Foundation International Data Journalism Award finalist.

My academic background is in mathematics, statistics, and environmental science, and I've taught at UBC. That quantitative foundation, combined with years of executive experience in a data-driven business, is what led me toward data strategy: I understand both the business questions worth asking and the technical work required to answer them.
